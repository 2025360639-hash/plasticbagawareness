Plastic Bag Awareness

Learn about the environmental impact of plastic bags and how you can help protect our planet.

Why Plastic Bags Are Harmful

Plastic bags are widely used because they are cheap and convenient. However, they can take hundreds of years to decompose and contribute to pollution on land and in the ocean.

Plastic bags take hundreds of years to decompose.

Environmental Pollution

Plastic waste pollutes rivers, oceans and public areas.

Plastic bags can harm marine animals.

Affected Wildlife

Animals may accidentally eat plastic waste or become trapped, causing injury and death.

Reusable bags help reduce plastic waste.

Reusable Alternatives

Using reusable bags reduces plastic consumption and helps protect natural resources.
