Who We Are

Plastic Bag Awareness is an educational website created to encourage people to reduce plastic bag usage and adopt reusable bags in their daily lives.

Our Vision

To create a cleaner and greener environment through sustainable shopping habits.

Our Mission

  • Promote reusable shopping bags.
  • Educate communities about plastic pollution.
  • Support environmental sustainability.

Plastic Bag Awareness

Together we can reduce plastic waste and build a cleaner, greener future for everyone.
